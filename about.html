About Me

I manage 6 Code Nation programs in San Francisco and Oakland. I love hiking, baseball, and yoga.

I grew up in Fresno, CA and attended UC Santa Cruz. I studied Linguistics and Computer Science. At the time, I was curious about dialogue systems like Siri and Alexa and how they worked. I have always loved volunteering and giving back to my community. I wanted to pursue a meaningful career so I became a teacher. I taught secondary math in Las Vegas, NV for three years before learning about Code Nation. I wanted to increase my impact and break into computer science education.
